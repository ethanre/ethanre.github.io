Movie Review

Kingsman: The Secret Service

Kingsman is a James Bond-esque movie with many action scenes and undercover missions.

The movie is set in England and the United States. The main character, Eggsy is recruited into the Kingsman, a secret spy organization that pretends it's a tailor. Eggsy and his friends are trying to stop an evil Samuel L. Jackson from taking over the world.

Rating of Kingsman

I would rate Kingsman a solid four out of five stars.

The movie was very entertaining at the least, but it had its brutal as well as its ridiculous moments. Overall it was a very enjoyable and entertaining movie.
